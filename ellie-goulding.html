Ellie Goulding (Singer-Songwriter) - On This Day

Full Name: Elena Jane Goulding
Profession: Singer-Songwriter

Biography: Ellie Goulding is an English singer, songwriter and record producer. She rose to fame in 2010 after her debut album Lights topped the UK albums chart and spawned a number of hit singles, including "Starry Eyed" and "Lights."

She has released four more studio albums, including Delirium (2015) and Brightest Blue (2020). She has also achieved success with soundtracks for films like Fifty Shades of Grey (2015) and Rocketman (2019).

Goulding began her musical career in 2009, when she released her first EP, An Introduction to Ellie Goulding. Her debut album, Lights, was released in March 2010 and was later certified triple platinum in the UK and double platinum in the US.

Since then, Goulding has gone on to sell over 30 million albums and 100 million singles worldwide and won a number of awards, including two Brit Awards, four MTV Europe Music Awards, and a Billboard Music Award.
