Nightbirde's Family Submits Her Iconic Single 'It's OK' For a Grammy Award

TALENTRECAP.COM Talent America's Got Talent

Trae Patton/NBC

Reading Time: 3 minutes

The late and beloved singer-songwriter, Nightbirde, has left an unforgettable mark on the music industry. Recently, her family and team took a significant step to honor her legacy. They submitted her iconic single, “It’s OK,” for consideration at the Grammy Awards.

Nightbirde’s “It’s OK” Is Submitted The “Song for Social Change” Grammy Award

On October 18, Nightbirde’s family revealed that “It’s OK” is now submitted for the Grammy Award for “Song For Social Change.” The news came along with a touching video of the deceased singer performing it in a recording studio.

After rising to fame on America’s Got Talent, Nightbirde’s hit “It’s OK” had an impact on listeners around the world. This song offers encouragement to those facing any type of adversity. On top of the song’s meaning, Nightbirde’s cancer journey made her a powerful symbol of both hope and resilience. For those who may not know, Nightbirde passed away on February 19, 2022.

In the video’s caption, the singer’s team shared a glimpse into her thoughts and aspirations. They revealed that she had recorded multiple versions of her Grammy speech and had been diligently journaling her thoughts.

In her recorded speech, Nightbirde reflects on her hope, a hope that never wavered, even in the face of hardship. She clung to that hope despite repeated setbacks, and it always found a way to return.

“‘It’s OK’ has been a ray of hope that has touched the hearts of people from every corner of the globe,” her family wrote. “The decision to submit ‘It’s OK’ for the ‘Song for Social Change’ Grammy award is certainly a fitting tribute to Nightbirde’s enduring impact.”

The Nightbirde Foundation Will Host a Fundraiser Event Featuring Dustin Tavella

The deceased singer’s family and team continue to carry her legacy forward through the Nightbirde Foundation. In a recent Instagram post, they announced a special event on her behalf. This event promises to be a night of magic and hope. Participants will celebrate her life and contribute to the causes she held dear.

The poster, shared on Nightbirde’s Instagram, features the details of this special event, titled “Nightbirde Night of Magic and Hope.” The memorial fundraiser aims to support the Nightbirde Foundation. For those who may not know, the Nightbirde Foundation helps bring hope to women with breast cancer.

Notably, the event will feature a performance by America’s Got Talent Season 16 winner Dustin Tavella. As the caption notes, this is a show you won’t want to miss, and for a good cause at that.

The “Night of Magic and Hope” is on Friday, October 27, with doors opening at 6 p.m. This special event is an opportunity for friends, fans, and supporters of Nightbirde to come together. Audience members will not only celebrate her life, but contribute to the causes that mattered to her most.
