Superhero Movies To Anticipate In 2014

This is a sequel to the X-men First Class

2. Captain America : The Winter Soldier

The first Captain America movie was a terrible movie for me considering i had high hopes for it, but seeing that the Avenger Movie was a huge success we can only hope that they have improved upon their movie making skills and awe us next year. Chris Evans remains the hero and Scarlett Johansson and Samuel Jackson will also be cast for the upcoming movie next April.

3. The Amazing Spiderman 2

The amazing spiderman movie came out in 2012 with Emma Stone and real life boyfriend Andrew Garfield, it was undisputedly one of my best movies that year. As opposed the the series with Tobey Maguire, i think Andrew Garfield played a better Peter Parker/ Spiderman superhero. Also changing focus from Mary Jane Watson to Gwen Stacy was an excellent move. In the upcoming sequel, the lead stars will remain and Jamie Foxx will be an addition to the cast as he plays a villian; Electro.
