Blake Shelton recently announced on his Instagram that his USA network game show Barmageddon is currently casting for Season 2. The show is looking for people with “internet fail videos” to appear on the show, which Shelton created with The Voice host Carson Daly.

Blake Shelton’s Barmageddon Casting for Season 2

Barmageddon is filmed at Shelton’s Nashville bar Ole Red. It features celebrities competing against each other in amped-up versions of bar games. The celebrities are paired with regular people who went viral for something embarrassing.

The show is currently looking for Season 2 contestants who have had their own fails caught on video. Contestants must be 21 or older. You can visit the casting website to fill out the form to be considered. You’ll need to provide your name, contact information, and social media links.

Applicants are also expected to share their viral fail videos and explain what happened. The form also asks applicants to share three celebrities they’d like to meet, as well as three fun facts about themselves. They’re also asked to share dates they would be unavailable between April 23 and May 10, 2023.

The Show Has Featured Gwen Stefani, Brie Bella, and More

Barmageddon premiered in December on the USA network and aired several episodes into the new year. Shelton was a contestant in the first episode, competing against fellow country star Kane Brown. In the second episode, Shelton’s wife Gwen Stefani competed against Sheryl Crow.

The show is hosted by WWE star Nikki Bella. Her twin sister Brie Bella was a celebrity contestant on one of the episodes. Other celebrities who appeared during the first season included SNL star Jay Pharoah, country singer Trace Adkins, and actress Malin Akerman.
