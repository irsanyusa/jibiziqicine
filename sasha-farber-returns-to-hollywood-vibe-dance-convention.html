Sasha Farber Returns to Hollywood Vibe Dance Convention

Dancing With The Stars professional dancer Sasha Farber has returned to Hollywood Vibe, a popular dance convention, and competition. The announcement was made on Hollywood Vibe’s Instagram page, where they shared a photo of Farber along with the words, “Welcome Back Sasha Farber!”

Sasha Farber Returns to Hollywood Vibe

Sasha Farber has been a regular presence at Hollywood Vibe over the years, often serving as a guest teacher and judge. His return to the convention is sure to excite dancers and fans alike, who have come to admire his talent and passion for dance.

Farber’s experience and expertise in the dance industry make him a valuable asset to Hollywood Vibe, where he will be sharing his knowledge and skills with the next generation of dancers. With his return, dancers can look forward to learning from one of the best in the business and getting a taste of the magic that makes Hollywood Vibe such a special event.

During his time on DWTS, Farber has choreographed memorable routines for celebrities such as Simone Biles, Tonya Harding, and Mary Lou Retton. He has also appeared as a dancer on several seasons of the show, partnering with celebrities such as Nicole “Snooki” Polizzi, Terra Jolé, and Justina Machado.

Overall, Farber’s return to Hollywood Vibe is a testament to his dedication to dance and his commitment to inspiring the next generation of dancers. With his return, dancers can look forward to learning from one of the best in the business and getting a taste of the magic that makes Hollywood Vibe such a special event.

Sasha Farber Going Through Divorce With Emma Slater

Farber announced his involvement with Hollywood Vibe, but he is currently going through a divorce from his wife and fellow professional dancer Emma Slater.

Farber and Slater announced their divorce in August 2022, and the couple, who had been together for a long time, has described it as a difficult time. Despite the challenges, fans have remained supportive of the couple throughout their split, with many leaving encouraging comments on Farber’s Instagram posts.

In the face of this personal challenge, Farber has continued to focus on his work and his passion for dance. His involvement in Hollywood Vibe, as well as his upcoming appearance on the next season of DWTS prove his resilience and dedication to his craft.
