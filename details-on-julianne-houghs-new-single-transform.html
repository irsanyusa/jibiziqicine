We all know Julianne Hough is an amazing dancer, but did you know she can sing too? She’s showing off her talent to the world with her new single, “Transform”!

Julianne Hough’s Underrated Musical Talents

“Transform is Julianne’s Latest Music-Related Project

“Transform” was released a few weeks ago and is all about staying positive and true to who you are while also constantly trying to be a better version of yourself, as detailed in the song’s lyrics.

Julianne performed the song on the America’s Got Talent Season 14 finale with finalists V Unbeatable. It’s already racked up over a million views on YouTube!

Julianne Hough Has a Long History in the Music Industry

“Transform” isn’t Julianne’s first foray into the music industry: she was part of a 3-person singing group called 2B1G – that stands for Two Boys One Girl – when she was studying the performing arts in Britain. (Fun fact: they actually once auditioned for Julianne’s now-fellow AGT judge Simon Cowell!) She later released a self-titled solo album, Julianne Hough, and has come out with various singles every once and awhile.

And, of course, her most prominent music-related venture was her role in FOX’s Grease Live! as Sandy back in 2016.
