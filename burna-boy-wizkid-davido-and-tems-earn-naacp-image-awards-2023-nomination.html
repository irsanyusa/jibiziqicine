Burna Boy, Wizkid, Davido, and Tems earn NAACP Image Awards 2023 nomination

Wizkid and Davido were also nominated for Best International Song for 'Bad To Me' and 'Stand Strong'.

Tems also earned a nomination for Best Hip Hop Song for her contributions on Future's 'Wait For U'.

The 2023 NAACP Image Award is set to hold on Saturday, February 25, 2023.
