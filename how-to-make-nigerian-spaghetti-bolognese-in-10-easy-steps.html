Here are the steps to making your bolognese sauce;

  • Pour vegetable oil into a frying pan and let it heat up for a bit.
  • Pour ginger and onions into the teeming oil and let it fry for a few seconds.
  • Then add the minced meat, stir it for a bit.
  • Add seasoning, thyme, curry and salt and stir.
  • Allow it to cook for 10 minutes.
  • Add the blended tomato, pepper and tomato paste.
  • Pour the meat stock water in.
  • Add spring onions and carrots.
  • Let it cook for a few minutes.
  • Then boil your spaghetti.

    • Your spaghetti bolognese is ready.
