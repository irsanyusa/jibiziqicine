Amtrak Train Jumps Tracks in North Carolina, Injuring at Least 55

published on August 28, 2024
An Amtrak train derailed Monday in Halifax County, North Carolina, toppling one of its cars and injuring at least 55 people. The train’s conductor and at least 54 passengers were among the injured, the Associated Press reported. Of those taken to the hospital following the accident, one was seriously injured while the rest escaped with non-life threatening injuries, according to officials.
