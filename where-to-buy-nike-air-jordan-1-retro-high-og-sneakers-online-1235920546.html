For Michael Jordan, he took care of business on the basketball court. It’s where he walked the walk and put his money where his mouth is, as he strived for greatness. However, for those who aren’t Michael Jordan, Nike offers up new limited-edition Air Jordan 1 Retro High OGs to “stand on business,” or to take care of what needs to be done.

Men’s sizes are priced at $180 at nike.com, the Nike Air Jordan 1 Retro High OG sneakers for adults has an instantly iconic and classic silhouette. It has a refined and elegant black and white colorway to take care of your own business and standout in the grind.

$180Buy: Nike Air Jordan 1 Retro High OG

Design with premium leather, the Nike Air Jordan 1 Retro High OG sneakers also features Nike Air-Sole for lightweight cushioning and solid rubber outsoles for traction — all with Nike’s eye-catching stitched-down Swoosh logo in white that matches its midsole with black tip, mudguard and quarter.
