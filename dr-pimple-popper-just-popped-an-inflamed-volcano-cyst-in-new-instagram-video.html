Dr. Pimple Popper Just Popped An Inflamed Volcano Cyst In New Instagram Video

In her latest Instagram video, Dr. Pimple Popper pops an inflamed cyst in a mystery location on her patient. The doctor captioned the video: "An inflamed cyst to squeeze into the weekend! #drpimplepopper #popaholicsunite"

The Instagram video starts by panning over the bump. The cyst looks like a bruise on the top of the skin. It is a very deep shade of purple, and looks painful. Dr. Pimple Popper doesn't waste any time and gets right in there with her fingers. Right away, the squeeze is on. When she first starts squeezing she says, "It's still pretty solid."

She's right, Dr. Pimple Popper squeezes and out comes ribbons of solid goo. The doctor cleans up the skin around the opening and goes back for more squeezing. Each time, more goo squishes out, thick and gloppy. One Popaholic commented, "looks like when they make churros."

Dr. Pimple Popper says, "It's like a Christmas present." (That wasn't on the wish list.) She continues her routine of squeezing and cleaning until pop, the cyst gushes forth in a creamy waterfall. One commenter shared: "Its like a freakin volcano I love it."

Thankfully, she had a bowl placed underneath to catch everything hidden inside.
