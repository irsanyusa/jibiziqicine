Taylor Swift stole the show on the red carpet in a sparkly sequined romper with $546 butterfly heels

The "Delicate" singer stole the show when she arrived at the iHeart Radio Music Awards on Thursday wearing a shimmering romper by Rose Bloom . The romper retails for 185 (approximately $246 USD) and is still available in a size small (a UK 10-12, or US 6-8) at the time of writing.

Her romper was covered in iridescent purple sequins that sparkled as she made her way down the carpet. The cape sleeves added an extra touch of flowy elegance.

ADVERTISEMENT

Swift paired the glittering romper with an equally eye-catching pair of shoes. Her $546 heels by Sophia Webster had a playful butterfly design at the heel that showcased the colorful and whimsical style Swift has become known for.

Swift's video for "Delicate" ended up taking home the trophy for best music video, while her "Reputation Stadium Tour" won for best tour.

ADVERTISEMENT

This isn't the first time Swift has worn a romper on the red carpet, as she previously wore a blue design by Mary Katrantzou to the 2014 MTV Video Music Awards.

Swift's rompers may have been show-stoppers, but she's not the only celebrity to prove that rompers can be fit for the red carpet.

Rita Ora recently wore a flowing caped romper by Ashi Couture to the VH1 Trailblazer Honors in February.

ADVERTISEMENT

"Bumblebee" star Hailee Steinfeld opted for a glamorous version by Elie Saab for the 2016 American Music Awards.

Kristen Stewart went with an eye-catching floral print by Zuhair Murad for the "American Ultra" premiere in August 2015.

ADVERTISEMENT

If Swift's latest appearance is anything to go by, it looks like rompers might join jumpsuits and snakeskin designs as a major red-carpet trend in 2019.
