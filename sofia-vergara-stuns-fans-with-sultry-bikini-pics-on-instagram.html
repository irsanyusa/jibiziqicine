Sofia Vergara Stuns Fans With Sultry Bikini Pics on Instagram

America’s Got Talent judge Sofia Vergara bade farewell to 2022 in an alluring and fashionable account. Days ahead of the new year, the Colombian beauty filled her Instagram feed with photos of her wearing sizzling bikinis. Consequently, fans went crazy over these posts, fascinated by the fact that Vergara is already on her 50s.

Sofia Vergara Flaunts Her Curves During Tropical Vacation

The Modern Family actress has spent the holidays in a beach with her husband Joe Manganiello. As seen on Instagram, Vergara is indeed having fun during the lush trip. She uploaded a series of posts showing her romantic dates with Manganiello, her dainty OOTDs, and hot bikini pics.

Fans are definitely pleased to see that Vergara is enjoying relaxing time, but what amazed them the most are her swimsuits snaps. Last December 29, the AGT judge posted a mirror selfie while wearing a black two-piece swimsuit. With this photo came several praises in the comment section.

“She’s hot for a 20 year old and she’s 50??!!!?” one person wrote. “No frigging way.”

Aside from a holiday getaway, Vergara and her husband went to the beach to celebrate Manganiello’s 46th birthday. Yet, the actress seemed to have harmlessly gotten the attention with her sultry getups.

Fans Went Wild Upon Seeing Her Swimsuit Photos

The black bikini is not the only beach attire that Vergara took snaps of. Hours before 2023 kicks off, Vergara confidently donned a white one-piece swimsuit and posted it on Instagram. She also posed for another bikini shot, wearing a Dolce & Gabbana one-piece.

“Them curves,” one user commented.

One of Vergara’s followers noticed how her hair matches her swimsuits. Meanwhile, most comments express amusement over her seemingly ageless body.

“If she is 50 years old maybe I’m 85,” another person wrote.

The America’s Got Talent judge has obviously kicked off the new year warmly. Nonetheless, it seems that Vergara came back to work almost immediately, given her subsequent Instagram posts.
