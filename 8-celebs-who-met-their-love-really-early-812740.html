8 Celebs Who Met Their Love REALLY Early

Gender plays a role in the age at which people generally find their life partners, as revealed by research. On average, women tend to discover their partners at the age of 25, while men typically find their soulmates at the age of 28. Nevertheless, statistics don’t dictate the course of our lives, and numerous celebrities from our article found their true love before they even turned 20.

1. Sam Taylor-Johnson and Aaron Taylor-Johnson

Aaron Taylor-Johnson, the actor, and Sam Taylor-Johnson, the director, have been together for over a decade after meeting on the set of the 2009 film Nowhere Boy. Despite a significant age difference of 23 years, the couple has openly discussed their long-lasting love in numerous interviews. According to reports, Aaron was only 18 years old when they first met, and the duo tied the knot when he turned 19.

2. Tina Kunakey and Vincent Cassel

Actor Vincent Cassel and model Tina Kunakey first met during a vacation in Ibiza, where they started a holiday romance. Tina was only 19 at that time, while Vincent was already 49 years old. However, their age gap didn’t become a barrier to a serious relationship. The couple married several years later and gave birth to Amazonia Cassel, their daughter, in 2019.

3. Kylie Jenner and Travis Scott

Gilbert Flores/Broadimage/Broad Image/East News, Invision/Invision/East News

Kylie Jenner and Travis Scott began their relationship in 2017 and since then, their romantic journey has been well-documented in the media. Kylie was 19 years old when they first got together, while Travis was 24. In the same year, Jenner announced that she was pregnant, and the duo now have 2 children together.

4. Melanie Griffith and Don Johnson

AP/East News, Evan Agostini/Invision/AP/East News

The parents of Dakota Johnson, Melanie Griffith and Don Johnson, met when Melanie was 14 years old and Don was 22. They met on the set of their movie, The Harrad Experiment, which Griffith’s mother also starred in. Later she said that she felt “sheer panic” when she learned that her teenage daughter had fallen for an older man. The couple married each other twice but eventually split for good.

5. Jay-Z and Beyoncé

According to one of her interviews, Beyoncé was 18 years old when she first met Jay-Z. Although they began dating a year later, she had made a pact with herself — she was not going to get married before the age of 25, which she followed. In 2008, the couple got married in a private ceremony that was emotional and spiritual. The duo is now raising 3 children.

6. Sylvester Stallone and Jennifer Flavin Stallone

Sylvester Stallone and his wife, Jennifer Flavin first met in 1988. At the time, Stallone was 42 years old and had been divorced twice before. Flavin, on the other hand, was a 19-year-old model. Despite their 23-year age difference, it didn’t stop them from starting to build a family in 1997. And despite temporary splits, the couple seems to still be happy together.

7. Justin Bieber and Hailey Bieber

One of the most Instagram-famous couples on Earth, Justin and Hailey Bieber, are going to celebrate their fifth wedding anniversary this year. Although the duo first met in childhood, they reportedly started dating in 2015 when Justin was 21 and Hailey was just 19 years old. Although their relationship was not perfect in the beginning, with occasional splits, the duo decided to make their love a priority.

8. Millie Bobby Brown and Jake Bongiovi

Millie Bobby Brown, the 19-year-old actress known for her role in Stranger Things, just recently announced her engagement to her boyfriend, Jake Bongiovi. The couple has been dating for 2 years and has decided to not wait long to tie the knot. The actress revealed that she and her fiancé first connected on Instagram and became friends through the platform before their friendship blossomed into a romantic relationship.
