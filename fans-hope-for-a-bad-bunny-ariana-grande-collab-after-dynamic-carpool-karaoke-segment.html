Fans Hope For a Bad Bunny, Ariana Grande Collab After Dynamic 'Carpool Karaoke' Segment

Reading Time: 2 minutes

Puerto Rican rapper Bad Bunny has made headlines once again, but this time it’s for his love of Ariana Grande’s music. During a recent appearance on Carpool Karaoke with James Corden, Bad Bunny sang along to Grande’s 2014 hit “Break Free,” featuring Zedd, and even claimed it was the English song he knows best.

Bad Bunny Sings Ariana Grande’s ‘Break Free’ in Carpool Karaoke

In the video segment, the two men are seen driving around, enjoying themselves and singing along to different songs. However, when “Break Free” comes on, Bad Bunny truly shines. He enthusiastically sings along to the lyrics, even interrupting a conversation with Corden to do so.

As the chorus hits, both Bad Bunny and Corden become full-fledged concert performers, belting out the lyrics with gusto. It’s clear that Bad Bunny is a true fan of Grande’s music and his infectious energy has earned him even more fans.

During the car ride, Bad Bunny also took the opportunity to give Corden a taste of the pro wrestling world. He enlisted the help of future WWE Hall of Famer Rey Mysterio to teach Corden some moves inside the ring. The duo also discussed a variety of topics, such as Bad Bunny‘s rise to fame and how he chose his stage name.

Despite all the fun moments in the video, it’s clear that Bad Bunny’s love for Grande‘s music was the highlight. The rapper’s ability to sing along flawlessly to an English song is a testament to his versatility as a musician.

Fan are Begging The Two Artists to Collaborate

Ever since Bad Bunny’s appearance on Carpool Karaoke where he sang Grande‘s hit “Break Free,” fans have been begging for a collaboration between the two superstars. Social media has been buzzing with excitement, and fans have taken to Twitter to express their hopes for a collaboration.

“Bad Bunny and Ariana Grande need to collab ASAP. The way he sang ‘Break Free’ has me shook!” One fan tweeted.

“I need Bad Bunny and Ariana Grande to collaborate, their voices together would be magical,” Another fan wrote.

Undoubtedly, a collaboration between the two would produce a massive hit. Bad Bunny is known for his unique sound that blends Latin trap, reggaeton, and other genres, while Grande is a pop powerhouse with an impressive vocal range. The possibilities for a collaboration are endless.

Although there has been no official confirmation of a collaboration between the two artists, fans are hopeful that it will happen soon. In the meantime, they are enjoying Bad Bunny’s rendition of “Break Free,” and eagerly anticipating what the future holds for these two talented musicians.
