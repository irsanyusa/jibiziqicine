Meaning of One Time Password (OTP) in banking and how to register for it online

published on June 25, 2024
One of the most popular transaction protection methods today is two-factor authentication, commonly known as 2FA. One of the ways 2FA works is through an OTP (One-time PIN). When active, an OTP must be keyed in before a transaction is completed. The security feature is based on the premise that only an authorized user can be in possession of the device to which the OTP is sent.
