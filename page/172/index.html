published on July 13, 2024
April 16, 2012 4:34 PM EDT Columbia University has announced the 2012 Pulitzer Prize winners — and they include Afghan photographer Massoud Hossaini, whose picture of a girl reacting to a suicide bombing took the title in the category of breaking news photography. The explosion of which the young girl, Tarana Akbari, is a survivor killed more than 70 people.
