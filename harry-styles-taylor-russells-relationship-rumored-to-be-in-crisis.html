Harry Styles and his rumored girlfriend Taylor Russell might be going through a rough patch, according to new gossip. Russell reportedly didn’t see Styles during a recent visit to the UK, and their relationship is said to be “in crisis.”

Harry Styles, Taylor Russell’s Romance ‘In Crisis’?

Hollywood Life reports that, according to The Sun, Styles’ romance with Russell is apparently “in crisis” after the actress failed to see him during a recent UK visit. Russell, who was in London for a fashion shoot, apparently stayed at a hotel a few miles away from Styles’ house.

“Taylor spent her trip to London alone and stayed at a hotel instead of with Harry,” a source told The Sun. “It’s been a whirlwind romance but they’re still young and at pivotal points in their careers. They have a lot of respect for each other and had been planning on spending Christmas together.”

Styles and Russell were previously seen attending a Las Vegas U2 concert together in November. Styles also debuted his new buzz cut during this outing. Rumors started swirling that the two were a couple back in June of this year.

The Couple Was First Linked This Summer

Styles and Russell have not gone public with their romance, but the former One Direction star has been linked to the Canadian actress since June of this year. According to People, Russell attended one of Styles’ concerts, and Styles attended the opening of Russell’s play.

In October, a source told Us Weekly that Styles is “head over heels” for Russell, adding that “despite their busy schedules, they make time for each other as often as possible.” The source described their relationship as “very serious,” sharing that “Harry definitely sees a future with Taylor.”

Styles previously dated Olivia Wilde, but the pair broke up last year. Meanwhile, Russell was previously linked to fellow actor Lucas Hedges.
