Olivia Rodrigo Extends The &quot;GUTS&quot; 2024 World Tour

TALENTRECAP.COM Entertainment Celebrity

Photo by Theo Wargo/Getty Images for MTV

Reading Time: 2 minutes

Olivia Rodrigo is gearing up for a massive world tour in 2024. The pop star is set to headline a 57-date concert in support of her sophomore album, GUTS.

Olivia Rodrigo Announces GUTS World Tour, Additional Dates

In an Instagram post from September 13, Rodrigo enthusiastically announced the good news. Alongside the artwork for the tour, the singer also revealed dates which are set for next year.

“Soooo excited to announce the GUTS world tour!!!!” she wrote.

Produced by Live Nation, the trek will begin on February 23 at Acrisure Arena in Palm Springs, California. From there, she’ll perform in North America through April, where she’s bound to show up at Madison Square Garden in New York City for April 5 and 6. Rodrigo will then head overseas, kicking off her international dates on April 30 in Dublin, Ireland.

The “bad idea right?” singer will make further stops in Glasgow and Birmingham followed by two nights at The O2 in London. In May and June 2024, she will head to Europe and will make stops in Belgium, Denmark, Germany, Italy, and Spain. Later, she will return to the United States for a show in Philadelphia. Rodrigo will wrap her world tour with two concerts at the Kia Forum in Los Angeles on Aug. 13 and 14.

The Breeders, PinkPantheress, Chappell Roan and Remi Wolf will open on select dates.

The announced tour dates are only a fraction of the whole world tour. According to Rodrigo, fans should stay tune for “more dates coming soon.” Fans have already seen the addition of 18 more dates, and more could be on the way.

Registration for Access to Tickets is open now until Sunday, September 17.

Limited Number of Concert Tickets to Sell for Only $20

Intending to make her concert tickets more accessible for fans, Rodrigo is making a limited number of tickets more affordable at a later date. Certain number of tickets will sell for $20 (plus taxes and fees) under the Silver Star program.

These tickets are required to be bought in pairs with a limit of two tickets per purchase. Furthermore, the seats will be located next to each other, although the locations will be revealed on the day of the show. The same may be located on the floor, lower and upper levels, or limited view sections.

Aside from offering affordable tickets, Rodrigo is also launching the Fund 4 Good initiative. As per the press release, this has the objective of “building an equitable and just future for all women and girls through direct support of community-based non-profits that champion girls’ education, support reproductive rights and prevent gender-based violence.”
