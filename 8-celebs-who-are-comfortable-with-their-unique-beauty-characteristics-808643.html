8 Celebs Who Are Comfortable With Their Unique Beauty Characteristics

We all deal with imperfections in our own ways, whether it’s in the form of scars, acne, uneven teeth, or beauty marks. These characteristics make us all unique in our own way, and show how no 2 people can be the same. Even though we’re used to viewing celebrities as perfect beings, many of them have their own unique features as well that set them apart.

Bright Side is all about highlighting how imperfections are not something to be ashamed of but rather celebrated, so we collected a list of celebrities who have embraced their own distinct features.

1. Jason Momoa

Invision/Invision/East News

Jason Momoa, widely known for his role in Aquaman, is considered one of the most good-looking celebrities. One aspect of his appearance that has made him stand out from others is the facial scar he carries above his eyebrow. The actor has embraced it as a part of his appearance and is comfortable with it.

2. Whoopi Goldberg

Invision/Invision/East News

At first glance, many of us won’t be able to spot the unique characteristic that sets Whoopi Goldberg apart: her eyebrows. Goldberg, who reportedly does not like facial hair, chose to shave off her eyebrows and embrace this new look.

3. Anna Paquin

HBO / JOHNSON, JOHN P. / EAST NEWS, Dee Cercone/Everett Collection/EastNews

One of the things that made True Blood star Anna Paquin always stand out and look unique was her teeth gap, which the actress loves and is not insecure about. “I don’t really care about my teeth.” She said in an interview, choosing not to change this aspect about herself and setting a great example.

4. Kate Bosworth

Gilbert Flores /Broadimage/Broad Image/East News

Kate Bosworth is one of the few celebrities who possess the unique and very uncommon characteristic of heterochromia, or having 2 different-colored eyes. Bosworth has one blue and one hazel eye, and is not afraid of showing her eyes off.

5. Danny Trejo

What has made Danny Trejo instantly recognizable are the numerous scars on his face, which have set him apart from other celebrities. By embracing his appearance, the actor ended up receiving many roles and made them his trademark.

6. Kristen Stewart

Kristin Callahan/Everett Collection/EastNews

Kristen Stewart once stated that she is “uncomfortable about the size” of her ears, but this insecurity has not stopped the gorgeous actress in any way.

7. Laetitia Casta

Gys Danny/Reporters/ABACA/Abaca/East News

The gorgeous supermodel and actress Laetitia Casta has been very open about embracing her uneven teeth, stating that, “It is those tiny little imperfections, like my teeth, that make a model stand out.” She has resisted changing any part of herself for the industry, remaining true to her natural beauty.

8. Stephen Colbert

LISA O'CONNOR/AFP/East News

Stephen Colbert’s oddly shaped ear has been addressed by the talk show host multiple times, and it’s never been something he has been ashamed of. A surgery during his early years resulted in damage to his inner ear, causing his ear to stick out.

Is there any unique beauty characteristic on this list that you also share? Tell us your story in the comments.
