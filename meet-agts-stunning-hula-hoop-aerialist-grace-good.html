Meet 'AGT's Stunning Hula Hoop Aerialist Grace Good

Reading Time: 2 minutes

Prepare to be amazed by the stunningly sexy aerial act of Grace Good as she competes on America’s Got Talent Season 18. With over ten years of experience, she has captivated audiences worldwide as an extraordinary fire dancer, aerialist, and hula hoop artist.

Grace Good Combines Aerial Stunts With Hula Hoops

Grace is a multi-talented circus performer and instructor from Nashville, Tennessee, who is a force to be reckoned with. Recognized for her incredible skills, Grace has graced renowned stages like NASCAR’s Track Side Live at the Texas Motor Speedway. Her signature Hula Hoop Circus Ball act has earned international acclaim, leaving spectators in awe.

Recently, Grace announced that she will audition for season 18 of AGT, where she plans to showcase her unique talents to a wider audience. Her mesmerizing performances have already gained her a significant following on social media, with over 170 thousand Instagram followers and 41 million TikTok likes.

Beyond her captivating stage presence, Grace specializes in creating custom acts for various events such as halftime shows, grand openings, private affairs, and corporate events. From hula hoop dances to breathtaking fire performances and acrobatics, she offers a diverse range of unforgettable experiences.

Grace’s dedication to her craft extends to teaching others interested in mastering these incredible feats of skill and strength. She also loves to travel and explore new places while performing her acts around the world.

Grace Good Details Her Backstage Experience on AGT

In a recent interview, Grace shared her experience and the challenges she faced during her time on the show. Flying out to Los Angeles with her supportive boyfriend, Grace described the intense 12-hour day of filming.

She expressed a mix of nervousness and anticipation as she eagerly awaited her turn to perform. Safety was a crucial aspect of her performance, particularly when working with fire. At AGT and other events, dedicated safety teams and licensed pyrotechnics supported Grace, ensuring they took the proper precautions. Fire marshals often reviewed her act, and she emphasized the importance of having safety measures in place at all times.

Grace’s appearance on AGT marked the first time she performed her new routine in front of a live audience and also on TV. She revealed that the act contained daring stunts that she had never performed before, adding an extra element of excitement and risk to her performance.
