American Idol Season 20 contestant Christian Guardino shared an exciting announcement this week. The 21-year-old singer will be performing his first concert as a headliner in his hometown of Patchogue, New York, this November.

American Idol‘s Christian Guardino Announces First Headliner

Christian posted a video on Instagram Tuesday from the Patchogue Theatre, where he’ll be performing the concert on Friday, November 4. He shared his excitement about performing a concert in his hometown.

“Honestly, this has been a goal of mine for such a long time,” Christian said in the video, “and to say that I can have my very first headliner in my hometown is such an honor.”

Tickets for November’s show are available for pre-sale on Wednesday at noon with the promo code LOCAL22. The full sale of tickets will begin on Friday. Check out the Patchogue Theatre’s website for tickets and more information.

Christian Made It to the Top 7 on Season 20

Earlier his year, Christian made it to the Top 7 on American Idol Season 20. Although he didn’t make it to the Finals, he still got to release an original single called “Higher.” Christian also has several other singles available to stream, including “Poison Ivy” and “Wishful Thinking.”

Christian previously competed on America’s Got Talent in 2017, when he was 16 years old. He earned the Golden Buzzer from judge Howie Mandel for his audition to “Who’s Lovin’ You” by the Jackson 5. Christian made it to the Semifinals of Season 12.

Fellow Top 7 Idol contestant Jay Copeland also recently made a big announcement of his own, as he’s opening for music legend Patti LaBelle at a concert in Delaware this week. It just goes to show that you don’t have to be a finalist on the show to go far.
